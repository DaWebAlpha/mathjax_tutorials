  1. A binary operation \( \star \) defined on the set of real numbers, \( \mathbb{R} \) by \( x \star y = 2x + 2y - \frac{xy}{5} \). Find the:

    2. (a) Inverse of \( x \) under the operation \( \star \)

    (b) Truth set when \( m \star 7 = -2 \star m \)

    CLICK FOR SOLUTIONS

    Given: \(x \star y = 2x + 2y - \frac{xy}{5} \)

    Identity Element:

    Let \( e \) be the identity element such that \( x \star e = x \)

    \( 2x + 2e - \frac{xe}{5} = x \)

    Multiply through by 5:

    \( 10x + 10e - xe = 5x \)

    \( 10e - xe = 5x - 10x = -5x \)

    \( e(10 - x) = -5x \Rightarrow e = \frac{-5x}{10 - x} \)

    (a) Inverse of \( x \):

    Let the inverse of \( x \) be \( x^{-1} \) such that:

    \( x \star x^{-1} = e = \frac{-5x}{10 - x} \)

    Compute \( x \star x^{-1} \):

    \( 2x + 2x^{-1} - \frac{xx^{-1}}{5} = \frac{-5x}{10 - x} \)

    Multiply through by 5:

    \( 10x + 10x^{-1} - xx^{-1} = \frac{-25x}{10 - x} \)

    \( x^{-1}(10 - x) + 10x = \frac{-25x}{10 - x} \)

    \( x^{-1}(10 - x) = \frac{-25x}{10 - x} - 10x \)

    \( x^{-1}(10 - x) = \frac{-25x - 100x + 10x^2}{10 - x} = \frac{10x^2 - 125x}{10 - x} \)

    \( x^{-1} = \frac{10x^2 - 125x}{(10 - x)^2} \)

    Answer (a): \( \boxed{x^{-1} = \frac{10x^2 - 125x}{(10 - x)^2}} \)

    (b) Truth set when \( m \star 7 = -2 \star m \):

    \( m \star 7 = 2m + 2(7) - \frac{7m}{5} = 2m + 14 - \frac{7m}{5} \)

    \( -2 \star m = 2(-2) + 2m - \frac{(-2)m}{5} = -4 + 2m + \frac{2m}{5} \)

    Set both expressions equal:

    \( 2m + 14 - \frac{7m}{5} = -4 + 2m + \frac{2m}{5} \)

    Subtract \( 2m \) from both sides:

    \( 14 - \frac{7m}{5} = -4 + \frac{2m}{5} \)

    Multiply through by 5:

    \( 14 \times 5 - \frac{7m}{5} \times 5 = -4 \times 5 + \frac{2m}{5} \times 5 \)

    \( 70 - 7m = -20 + 2m \)

    \( 70 + 20 = 7m + 2m \Rightarrow 90 = 9m \Rightarrow m = 10 \)

    Answer (b): \( \boxed{\text{Truth set: } \{10\}} \)

  2. Given that \( x \)\( C \)\( 2 \) + \( x \)\( C \)\( 3 \) − \( 4x = 0 \), where \( x \) is a positive integer, find the value of \( x \).
    3. CLICK FOR SOLUTIONS

    We are given: \( {^x}C_2 + {^x}C_3 - 4x = 0 \)

    Recall the combinations formula:

    \( {^x}C_r = \frac{x!}{r!(x - r)!} \)

    \( \frac{x!}{(x - 2)!2!} + \frac{x!}{(x - 3)!3!} - 4x = 0 \)

    \( \frac{x(x - 1)(x -2)!}{(x -2)!2!} + \frac{x(x - 1)(x - 2)(x - 3)!}{(x - 3)!3!} - 4x = 0\)

    So,

    Multiply through by 6 to eliminate denominators:

    \( 3x(x - 1) + x(x - 1)(x - 2) - 24x = 0 \)

    Factor \( x(x - 1) \):

    \( x(x - 1)[3 + (x - 2)] - 24x = 0 \)

    \( x(x - 1)(x + 1) - 24x = 0 \)

    Factor out \( x \):

    \( x[(x - 1)(x + 1) - 24] = 0 \)

    \( x[x^2 - 1 - 24] = 0\)

    \( x(x^2 - 25) = 0 \)

    \( x^3 - 25x = 0 \)

    \( x(x^2 - 25)\)

    \( x = 0 \ or \ x^2 = 25 \)

    \( x = 0 \ or \ x = \pm 5\)

    Since x cannot be 0 or negative therefore x is 5

    Answer: \( \boxed{x = 5} \)

  3. A function f is defined by \( f(x) = \frac{72}{px + r}\) where p and q are constants. Given that \( f(6) = 12 \) and \( f(7) = 9 \) find;

    4. (a) The values of \( p \) and \( r \)

    (b) \( f^{-1}(\frac{5}{3}) \)

    CLICK FOR SOLUTIONS:

    \( f(x) = \frac{72}{px + r}, f(6) = 12, \ \text{and} \ f(7) = 9 \)

    Taking \( f(6) = 12 \rightarrow \frac{72}{p(6) + r} = 12 \)

    \( (6p + r) \times \frac{72}{6p + r} = 12 \times (6p + r) \)

    \( 72 = 12(6p + r) \)

    \( \frac{72}{12} = \frac{12(6p + r)}{12} \)

    \( 6 = 6p + r \)

    \( 6p + r = 6 \rightarrow \text{eqn (1)} \)

    Taking \( f(7) = 9 \rightarrow \frac{72}{p(7) + r} = 9 \)

    \( (7p + r) \times \frac{72}{7p + r} = 9 \times (7p + r) \)

    \( 72 = 9(7p + r) \)

    \( \frac{72}{9} = \frac{9(7p + r)}{9} \)

    \( 8 = 7p + r \)

    \( 7p + r = 8 \rightarrow \text{eqn (2)} \)

    Subtract eqn (1) from eqn (2):

    \( (7p + r) - (6p + r) = 8 - 6 \)

    \( p = 2 \)

    Substitute \( p = 2 \) into eqn (1):

    \( 6(2) + r = 6 \Rightarrow 12 + r = 6 \Rightarrow r = -6 \)

    Therefore, \( \boxed{p = 2}, \ \boxed{r = -6} \)

    To find \( f^{-1}\left( \frac{5}{3} \right) \):

    We have \( f(x) = \frac{72}{2x - 6} \)

    Let \( y = \frac{72}{2x - 6} \)

    \( y(2x - 6) = 72 \Rightarrow 2xy - 6y = 72 \Rightarrow 2xy = 72 + 6y \)

    \( x = \frac{72 + 6y}{2y} \)

    Now plug in \( y = \frac{5}{3} \):

    \( x = \frac{72 + 6 \cdot \frac{5}{3}}{2 \cdot \frac{5}{3}} = \frac{72 + 10}{\frac{10}{3}} = \frac{82 \cdot 3}{10} = \frac{246}{10} = 24.6 \)

    Therefore, \( \boxed{f^{-1}\left( \frac{5}{3} \right) = 24.6} \)

  4. Resolve \( \frac{2x - 7}{25x - 24 - 6x^{2}} \) into partial fractions
    5. CLICK FOR SOLUTIONS

    \(\frac{2x - 7}{25x - 24 - 6x^{2}} = \frac{2x - 7}{-6x^2 + 25x - 24}\)

    \(\frac{2x - 7}{-6x^2 + 25x - 24} = \frac{2x - 7}{(3x - 8)(2x - 3)}\)

    Let \(\frac{2x - 7}{(3x - 8)(2x - 3)} = \frac{A}{3x - 8} + \frac{B}{2x - 3}\)

    Combine the right-hand side into a single fraction:

    \(\frac{A(2x - 3) + B(3x - 8)}{(3x - 8)(2x - 3)}\)

    Now, equate numerators:

    \(2x - 7 = A(2x - 3) + B(3x - 8)\)

    Expand both terms on the right-hand side:

    \(A(2x - 3) = 2Ax - 3A\)

    \(B(3x - 8) = 3Bx - 8B\)

    So:

    \(2x - 7 = (2A + 3B)x + (-3A - 8B)\)

    Now, equate coefficients of like terms:

    Coefficient of \(x\): \(2 = 2A + 3B\)

    Constant term: \(-7 = -3A - 8B\)

    We now solve the simultaneous equations:

    • 1) \(2A + 3B = 2\)
    • 2) \(-3A - 8B = -7\)

    Multiply equation (1) by 3:

    \(6A + 9B = 6\)

    Multiply equation (2) by 2:

    \(-6A - 16B = -14\)

    Add both equations:

    \((6A - 6A) + (9B - 16B) = 6 - 14\)

    \(-7B = -8 \Rightarrow B = \frac{8}{7}\)

    Substitute \(B = \frac{8}{7}\) into equation (1):

    \(2A + 3 \cdot \frac{8}{7} = 2\)

    \(2A + \frac{24}{7} = 2\)

    \(2A = 2 - \frac{24}{7} = \frac{14 - 24}{7} = \frac{-10}{7}\)

    \(A = \frac{-5}{7}\)

    So the partial fraction decomposition is:

    \(\frac{2x - 7}{(3x - 8)(2x - 3)} = \frac{-5}{7(3x - 8)} + \frac{8}{7(2x - 3)}\)
